Privacy Policy – Presence Beep

Presence Beep is a Chrome extension designed to notify users with an audible and optional visual alert when a Poll or Quiz appears on Zoom Webinars pages.

Data Collection

Presence Beep does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Specifically:

No personally identifiable information is collected

No authentication data is collected

No financial or payment data is collected

No browsing history is collected

No analytics or tracking technologies are used

All processing occurs locally in the user’s browser.

Website Access

The extension operates only on Zoom Webinars pages in order to detect the presence of Polls or Quizzes and trigger an alert.
No page content is stored or transmitted outside the user’s browser.

Third Parties

Presence Beep does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties.

Remote Code

The extension does not execute remote code. All functionality is contained within the packaged extension.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
